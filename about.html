About the Fund

Our Mission

The Mission of the Mayor’s Fund for Philadelphia is to advance the Mayor's goals through leveraging public-private partnerships to the benefit of the citizens of Philadelphia.

Our Values

The Mayor’s Fund for Philadelphia (Board, Staff and Volunteers) is committed to fully integrating the following values into all that it does. These are the principles that will guide our work on behalf of the Mayor and for the residents of Philadelphia:

Stewardship

The Fund is committed to a superior level of stewardship of all public and private funds, with the highest standard of integrity, transparency and efficiency.

Equality

We seek to integrate equality in all that we do as an employer, funder, and in our role as a point of entry for public-private partnerships in Philadelphia.

Collaboration

The Fund will facilitate collaboration between Philadelphia’s public, private and nonprofit sectors to ensure a successful and prosperous Philadelphia.

Excellence

The Fund is committed to excellence in all that it does, and to always go the extra step for the residents of Philadelphia.

Our History

The Mayor’s Fund for Philadelphia was originally called the “Fund for Philadelphia” when it originated in the 1970s.
