Priorities & Goals

The Mayor’s Fund of Philadelphia supports programs and initiatives that improve the quality of lives for Philadelphians and align with one of the Mayor’s five strategic goals.

  • Education & Health

    Improve the health and education of Philadelphians.

  • Environment &Sustainability

    Philadelphia becomes the greenest and most sustainable city in the county.

  • Place of Choice

    Philadelphia is business friendly and people choose to live and stay in Philadelphia.

  • Public Safety

    People feel safer, and residents feel a greater responsibility to keep their neighborhoods safe.

  • Government Effectiveness & Efficiency

    Philadelphia government works efficiently and effectively, with integrity and responsiveness.
