A great investment in Philadelphia

Did you know that 95% of your contribution goes directly to the programs and services that improve the quality of life for all Philadelphians? That makes your contribution to the Mayor’s Fund for Philadelphia a great philanthropic investment in the people, places, and programs that make Philadelphia great.

Thank you for your support!

The Mayor's Fund for Philadelphia is a 501(c)(3) public charity; All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

If you would like to make your contribution by mail, please make check payable to:

The Fund for Philadelphia
City Hall Room 708
Philadelphia, PA 19107

Please note on the check if you would like your donation to be applied to a specific program/project (for example, Graduation Coaches).
